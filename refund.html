Refund Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

We want you to be happy with DateScan. If you're not, here's exactly how refunds work — no dark patterns, no hoops.

Refunds for Google Play Store Purchases

If you subscribed to Pro via the Google Play Store (Android app), refunds are handled by Google according to Google Play's refund policy, not by us. To request a refund for a Play Store purchase:

  • Open the Google Play order history.
  • Find the DateScan subscription and tap "Request a refund".
  • Alternatively: Play Store → your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → DateScan → Cancel or Refund.

Google generally allows self-service refunds within 48 hours of purchase; after that, refund decisions are made by Google on a case-by-case basis. We cannot override Google's decision on Play Store refunds, but we can support your case — email us with your Google Play order ID and we'll provide any information Google needs.

14-Day Goodwill Refund Window

Beyond Google Play's 48-hour self-serve window, we offer a discretionary 14-day goodwill refund if you're not satisfied — provided the service was not substantially used. To request one, email us (see below) and we'll coordinate the refund through Google Play on your behalf.

What "Substantially Used" Means

To keep things fair and prevent abuse, a purchase is considered "substantially used" — and therefore ineligible for a refund — when any of the following apply:

  • You have completed more than 10 scans after upgrading.
  • You have sent more than 20 AI Coach messages after upgrading.
  • You have used the Service for more than a total of 60 minutes of active session time.
  • You have already received a refund for a previous DateScan purchase.
  • The 14-day window has expired, counted from the moment of the original transaction.

Scans and messages used under the free tier (5 points per day, 1 point per scan or AI Coach message) do not count toward these limits.

EU Statutory Right of Withdrawal

Customers in the European Union also have a statutory right of withdrawal within 14 days under Directive 2011/83/EU. Because DateScan is digital content delivered instantly, this right may be waived at checkout once you begin using Pro features. If you have not yet used any Pro features, the statutory right applies in full and overrides the "substantially used" rule above.

How to Request a Refund

You have two options:

Option A — Self-serve via Google Play: Open the Google Play order history, find DateScan, and tap "Request a refund". This is the fastest path within the first 48 hours after purchase.

Option B — Contact us: If Google Play denies your refund or if you purchased more than 48 hours ago, DM us on any of our social channels (links in the footer) with "Refund Request" and include:

  • The Google account email used for the purchase
  • The plan you bought (Week, Month, or Year)
  • The Google Play order ID from your receipt (starts with GPA.)
  • A brief explanation of why you're requesting a refund

We aim to respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Google Play back to your original payment method and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days.

Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling a subscription stops future renewals but is not the same as a refund. When you cancel, you keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period and simply aren't charged again. To request money back, follow the refund steps above within the 14-day window.

Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us first. Initiating a chargeback with your bank without reaching out may result in permanent suspension of your Pro access. We'd much rather just issue the refund directly — it's faster for everyone.

No Partial Refunds for Active Periods

Once the 14-day window expires, we do not prorate partial refunds for unused days of an active Week, Month, or Year period. You keep Pro access until the end of the period, and simply aren't charged again.

Contact

Need a refund or have a billing question? Contact Google Play support directly via the Play Store for the fastest resolution, or DM us on any of our social channels (links in the footer).

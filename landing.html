Privacy by design

Your camera never leaves your phone.

Most "AI selfie" apps quietly upload your face to a cloud GPU. DateScan doesn't. The face landmarker, color analyzer and posture detector all run inside your browser via MediaPipe and WebAssembly. Your saved closet sits in your device's IndexedDB.

The optional AI Chat is the only feature that talks to a server, and it asks for explicit consent before sending an image. A built-in Private Mode disables even that and forces the on-device AI onto the CPU backend so no GPU fingerprint surface is touched.

Questions, answered

FAQ

Is my camera footage uploaded anywhere?

No. The face and outfit analysis runs entirely on your device using MediaPipe and WebAssembly. Your video frames never leave the app. The only feature that interacts with a server is the optional AI Style Coach Chat, and only after you provide explicit consent to process a specific message or image.

How do I get DateScan?

DateScan is available exclusively as an Android app.

Is it free?

Yes — the core scanner, action plan, and digital closet (up to three saved outfits) are completely free. Free users receive 5 points per day, where 1 point equals 1 scan or 1 AI Coach message. Pro unlocks unlimited scans, coaching, and closet space via secure Google Play billing.

What devices does it work on?

DateScan is designed for modern Android phones and tablets equipped with a camera. Simply download the app from the Google Play Store to get started.

Can I use it for video-call interviews?

Yes. Run DateScan right before you join the call — it specifically checks lighting direction, background tidiness, framing and posture, which is exactly what makes or breaks a Zoom-style first impression.

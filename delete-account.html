Delete your account

Immediate. No login required. Data is removed from our servers as soon as you submit.

This page lets you permanently delete all DateScan data linked to your anonymous account ID. You do not need to log in. This is the official account deletion URL we submit to the Google Play Store.

What gets deleted

When you submit the form below, we immediately erase from our servers:

  • Your AI Style Coach chat history, including any photos you attached
  • Any content moderation reports you submitted
  • Your subscription and entitlement records linked to your account ID
  • Server-side usage logs keyed to your account

What we can't delete from here

  • On-device data (closet, scan history, local settings) — this lives only on your phone or browser. Open the DateScan app → Privacy → "Delete My Account & Data" to wipe it (that button does both server and local deletion in one go), or simply uninstall the app.
  • Google Play payment records — handled by Google under their own retention rules. You can cancel your subscription on the Google Play subscriptions page. Google keeps billing records as long as required by tax law.
  • Backup snapshots — we keep encrypted backups for 35 days for disaster recovery. Your data will be purged from backups on the normal rotation schedule and will not be restored to any live system.

Cancel your subscription first

Deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription. You will keep being charged. Please cancel first:

  • Google Play subscription: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions

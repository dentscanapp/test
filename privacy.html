1. Who We Are

DateScan is an AI-powered style coach available as a web app at https://datescan.app/ and as an Android app distributed via the Google Play Store. For any privacy question, use the deletion form at datescan.app/delete-account.html with "Privacy Request" in the reason field, or DM us on the social channels linked in the footer.

2. Age Restriction — 18+ Only

DateScan is intended exclusively for users aged 18 or older. The Service is not directed at children or teenagers, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone under 18. By using DateScan you represent and warrant that you are at least 18 years of age. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has used DateScan, please contact us immediately at the address above.

3. How Camera Scans Work (On-Device)

When you run a scan, Google's MediaPipe face-landmark model is loaded into your browser and executes entirely on your device's own CPU and GPU via WebGL/WebAssembly. For the scan feature specifically, this means:

  • Your camera feed is processed frame-by-frame on your device.
  • Raw frames, biometric landmarks, and video are discarded the moment you leave the scan screen.
  • No image data from the scan feature is uploaded to our servers or to any third party.

4. How the AI Style Coach Chat Works (Server-Side)

The AI Style Coach chat is a separate feature from the on-device scan. When you send a message to the Style Coach — with or without an attached photo — the following happens:

  • Your message text, any attached image, and a short recent chat history are sent over HTTPS to our backend at api.datescan.app.
  • Our backend forwards the request to a third-party vision-language model provider (currently OpenAI and/or Anthropic, subject to change) so the model can generate a reply.
  • The reply is streamed back to your device.
  • We retain request logs (message content, image references, timestamp, anonymized user identifier) for up to 30 days for abuse prevention, debugging, and moderation. After 30 days the content is automatically deleted.
  • We do not use your chat content to train AI models. Our third-party providers operate under zero-retention and no-training agreements where available; please consult their policies below for details.
  • If you attach a photo, you are asked for explicit consent via an in-app modal before the upload happens. You can turn off all image uploads in chat by enabling Private Mode in the Privacy panel.

Do not upload photos of other people without their consent, and never upload photos of anyone under 18. You are solely responsible for the content you submit to the Style Coach.

5. Data We Store

5.1 Referral Program — Data Processing

DateScan offers a referral program that allows you to invite friends and earn bonus points. When you share your personal referral link (datescan.app/?ref=<yourUserId>), the following data processing occurs:

  • Referral link generation — Your anonymous device/user ID (datescan.userId in localStorage) is embedded into the referral link. This ID is a randomly generated string (e.g. ds_a1b2c3d4) and does not contain any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.
  • When someone opens your referral link — The ref parameter in the URL is read by the app on the new user's device. The app then sends a request to our backend (POST api.datescan.app/api/referral) containing only the referrer's anonymous user ID and the new user's anonymous user ID. No other personal data is transmitted.
  • Bonus points — Both the referrer and the new user receive +5 bonus points. Points are stored locally on each device in localStorage under datescan.points. Bonus points are tracked separately from daily free points.
  • Anti-abuse measures — Self-referral (using your own link) is blocked client-side. Duplicate referral crediting is prevented by storing a datescan.referredBy flag in localStorage. The backend also validates that a user ID cannot refer itself.
  • Server-side data — Our backend stores the referrer ID, new user ID, and timestamp for each successful referral. This data is retained for the purpose of preventing abuse and for analytics, and is deleted in accordance with the retention periods in §12.
  • URL cleanup — After processing the referral parameter, the app removes the ?ref= query string from the browser's address bar using history.replaceState, so the referrer's ID is not visible in the URL or browser history.
  • Your rights — You can delete your referral data at any time by requesting data deletion via the form at datescan.app/delete-account.html. Local referral data (the referredBy flag and bonus points) can be cleared by using the in-app "Erase everything on this device" option in the Privacy panel.

DateScan bietet ein Empfehlungsprogramm an, mit dem du Freunde einladen und Bonuspunkte verdienen kannst. Wenn du deinen persönlichen Empfehlungslink teilst (datescan.app/?ref=<userId>), erfolgt die folgende Datenverarbeitung:

  • Empfehlungslink-Generierung — Deine anonyme Geräte-/Benutzer-ID (datescan.userId im localStorage) wird in den Empfehlungslink eingebettet. Diese ID ist eine zufällig generierte Zeichenkette (z.B. ds_a1b2c3d4) und enthält keine persönlichen Daten wie Name, E-Mail oder Telefonnummer.
  • Wenn jemand deinen Link öffnet — Der ref-Parameter in der URL wird von der App auf dem Gerät des neuen Nutzers gelesen. Die App sendet dann eine Anfrage an unser Backend (POST api.datescan.app/api/referral), die nur die anonyme Benutzer-ID des Empfehlenden und die anonyme ID des neuen Nutzers enthält. Es werden keine weiteren persönlichen Daten übermittelt.
  • Bonuspunkte — Sowohl der Empfehlende als auch der neue Nutzer erhalten +5 Bonuspunkte. Punkte werden lokal auf jedem Gerät im localStorage unter datescan.points gespeichert. Bonuspunkte werden getrennt von den täglichen Gratiskontingent-Punkten erfasst.

  • Serverseitige Daten — Unser Backend speichert für jede erfolgreiche Empfehlung die Empfehlender-ID, die neue Benutzer-ID und den Zeitstempel. Diese Daten werden zum Zwecke der Missbrauchsprävention und für Analysen aufbewahrt und gemäß den in §12 genannten Aufbewahrungsfristen gelöscht.
  • URL-Bereinigung — Nach Verarbeitung des Empfehlungsparameters entfernt die App den ?ref=-Query-String aus der Adressleiste des Browsers mittels history.replaceState, sodass die ID des Empfehlenden nicht in der URL oder Browserverlauf sichtbar bleibt.
  • Deine Rechte — Du kannst deine Empfehlungsdaten jederzeit löschen, indem du eine Löschanfrage über das Formular auf datescan.app/delete-account.html stellst. Lokale Empfehlungsdaten (das referredBy-Flag und Bonuspunkte) können über die Option „Alles auf diesem Gerät löschen" im Datenschutz-Panel der App gelöscht werden.

On your device (local)

DateScan uses your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB to remember your progress and preferences between visits. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Onboarding completion state
  • Your XP, level, streak, and mission history
  • Your digital closet (photos you save yourself)
  • Your daily free-tier points balance (5 points per day — each scan or AI Coach message costs 1 point; the pool resets 24 hours after your first spend)
  • Whether you have an active Pro subscription
  • Your cookie-banner and privacy-panel choices

You can erase all of this at any time by opening the Privacy panel in the app and tapping "Erase everything on this device", or by clearing site data for datescan.app in your browser.

On our server

  • AI Style Coach chat logs — retained up to 30 days (see §4).
  • Content moderation reports — when you tap "Report" on an AI response, we store the reported text, your chosen category, and any optional note, retained for up to 12 months for safety auditing.
  • Server access logs — IP address, user agent, request path, timestamp, retained for up to 14 days for security, retained longer in aggregated/anonymized form for analytics.
  • Subscription/entitlement records — we store a minimal record linking your anonymized device/account ID to your Pro status, plan type, and renewal date for the purpose of gating premium features.

Payment data

We do not store full payment card details on our servers under any circumstance. All subscription payments are processed by Google Play Billing. Google collects the billing data and shares only minimal entitlement information with us (plan, renewal date, anonymized purchase token). See Google's Privacy Policy.

Infrastructure & server logs

Our static files are served via Cloudflare, which handles TLS termination, DDoS protection, caching, and abuse prevention. Cloudflare automatically records standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamps) in its own server logs for security purposes.

11. Data Deletion

You have two ways to delete your data:

  • In-app wipe (local data only): Open the Privacy panel inside DateScan and tap "Erase everything on this device". This wipes your closet, scan history, local settings, and cached service worker data from the current device.
  • Web-based account deletion (local + server data): Visit https://datescan.app/delete-account.html and submit the form. No login is required. We will permanently delete your chat logs, moderation reports, and entitlement records associated with your email/device identifier within 30 days. This URL is also the account deletion link we submit to the Google Play Store, in compliance with Google Play's account deletion policy.

Deleting your data does not cancel an active subscription. To cancel Pro, open the Google Play subscriptions page — see also our Refund Policy.

12. Data Retention

We retain different categories of data for different periods:

  • On-device data — kept on your device until you delete it.
  • AI Style Coach chat logs — up to 30 days for abuse prevention and debugging, then automatically deleted.
  • Content moderation reports — up to 12 months for safety auditing.
  • Server access logs — up to 14 days in raw form.
  • Subscription records — for the duration of the subscription plus the period required by applicable tax and accounting law (typically 7 years in the EU).
  • Billing records held by Google Play — as long as required by Google Play's policies and applicable tax law.

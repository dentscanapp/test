Common Issues

"My camera won't turn on"

Check that you've granted camera permission to datescan.app in your browser settings. On iOS, you may also need to go to Settings → Safari → Camera and set it to "Ask" or "Allow". Make sure no other app is currently using your camera. Try reloading the page.

"The scan gave me a weird result"

Lighting matters a lot. Face a window or a bright lamp — avoid backlighting yourself with a window behind you. Keep your face centered and about arm's length from the camera. If you're wearing a dark outfit against a dark background, the outfit color detection may struggle.

"I lost my Pro access after clearing cookies"

Pro access on DateScan is tied to your browser's local storage. If you cleared site data, restore your purchase from the in-app Settings → "Restore purchases" button, or email info@datescan.app with your Google Play order ID.

"I want a refund"

See our Refund Policy for details. Refunds on Google Play subscriptions are handled directly through your Play Store account within 48 hours of purchase.

"I have feedback or a feature idea"

We love feedback. Seriously. Email us at info@datescan.app. If your idea makes it into the product, we'll let you know.

"I'm a journalist / creator / partner"

Email info@datescan.app with "Press" or "Partnership" in the subject and we'll route it to the right person.

Privacy Requests & Data Deletion

To delete your data, you have two options:

  • Fastest — web form (no login required): email us at info@datescan.app with "Delete my data" in the subject. We'll permanently delete your chat logs, moderation reports, and subscription records within 30 days.
  • In-app wipe (local data only): Open the Privacy panel inside the app and tap "Erase everything on this device".

For other GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA requests (access, rectification, portability, objection), email info@datescan.app with your request. See our Privacy Policy for the full list of rights and how we handle them.

Reporting Harmful AI Content

If the AI Style Coach gave you a response that is offensive, dangerous, sexual, or otherwise inappropriate, email info@datescan.app with a screenshot of the message and a short description of what went wrong.

If you believe any content on DateScan may involve a minor or constitute illegal activity, use the subject line "URGENT — Safety" so we can escalate it to the top of the queue.
